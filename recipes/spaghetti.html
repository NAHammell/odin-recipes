Spaghetti

Description

An easy comforting meal that's ready in only 15 minutes.

Ingredients

  • One 12-ounce box of Barilla half-cut spaghetti
  • A pinch of salt, to taste
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 24 ounces of Barilla Tomato and Basil Sauce
  • Finely chopped basil for garnish, optional
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese for garnishing, optional

Steps

  1. Add the pasta and 3 cups of cold water to a pot, optional salt to taste, then boil over high heat until al dente.
  2. To a large skillet add the ground beef and cook over medium-high heat, breaking up the meat with a spatula as it cooks, until browned.
  3. After beef has cooked through add the pasta sauce, stir to combine, and cook for 1 to 2 minutes.
  4. After pasta has been cooked drain and plate pasta. Add sauce as desired.
  5. Optionally garnish with basil and Parmesan to taste then serve immediately.
