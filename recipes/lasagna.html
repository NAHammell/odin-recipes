Lasagna

Description

A simple but delicious recipe that any cook should know how to make.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 4 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 pound Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 36 ounces pasta sauce
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1/4th cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 egg, beaten

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Cook the pasta al dente. Rinse under cold water and set aside.
  2. Brown the beef, sausage, onion, and garlic over medium high heat until no pink remains. Drain any fat.
  3. Stir in pasta sauce, tomato paste, Italian seasoning. Simmer 5 minutes.
  4. Add Cheese Mixture by combining 1 1/2 cups mozzarella, 1/4th cup parmesan cheese, ricotta, parsley, and egg.
  5. Add 1 cup meat sauce to 9x13 pan. Top with 3 lasagna noodles. Layer with 1/3rd of Cheese Mixture and 1 cup of meat sauce. Repeat twice more. Finish with 3 noodles topped with remaining sauce.
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 45 minutes.
  7. Uncover, sprinkle with remaining cheese, and bake an additional 15 minutes or until browned and bubbly. Broil 2-3 minutes if desired.
  8. Rest 10-15 minutes before cutting.
